🤖 STEAM Robotics Outreach

My robotics outreach program is designed to spark curiosity and creativity in young learners across Ghana. Through hands-on kits, workshops, and mentorship, I help students explore the intersection of science, technology, engineering, arts, and math—building not just robots, but confidence and community.

🔧 What I Build

  • ✔️ DIY robotics kits for classroom and home use
  • ✔️ Interactive coding sessions using sensors and motors
  • ✔️ STEAM workshops with real-world challenges
  • ✔️ Mentorship and storytelling to inspire future innovators

🌱 Impact

These programs have reached dozens of students, helping them build their first robots, understand core engineering principles, and dream bigger. It’s not just about tech—it’s about transformation.
