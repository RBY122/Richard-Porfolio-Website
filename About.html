About Me

I’m Richard Boakye Yiadom, an aspiring IT educator, data analyst, and product developer from the Ashanti Region of Ghana. My work is deeply rooted in creating practical and impactful systems—whether it’s optimizing blood donation logistics through BloodBridge, streamlining school operations with Scholar, or inspiring young minds through STEAM education.

“Empowering communities through education and technology.”
